Support

Help: For technical support or questions, email "spaghetti@miramontes.dev". To verify ownership of your account, you may be asked to provide a verification token from the "Settings" page.

Account Deletion: To request a deletion of your account and all associated information, email "spaghetti@miramontes.dev". Include your verification token from the "Settings" page and the words "Delete my Spaghetti Account" in your email. Once your account is deleted, it is unrecoverable. Your spaghetti transaction history will be deleted, but your transactions on other user's transaction logs will remain.
